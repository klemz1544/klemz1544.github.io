Lore

The Originium

The Originium is a mysterious crystal that has the power to manipulate reality itself. It is the source of all life in the world of Arknights and can be used to create powerful weapons and magical artifacts. However, those who are exposed to too much Originium can become infected with a deadly disease known as Oripathy, which can cause them to mutate into monstrous creatures.

The Infected

Those who are infected with Oripathy are known as the Infected. They can manifest a variety of different powers depending on the type and severity of their infection. Some Infected can control fire, while others can manipulate gravity or even create illusions.

The Operators

The Operators are a group of warriors who are tasked with protecting the world from the Infected. They are highly skilled in combat and are equipped with powerful weapons and abilities. Each Operator has their own unique personality and backstory, and they all play an important role in the ongoing battle against the Infected.
